  • Surveillance - where is the minefield?
  • Galaxy and stars - separating into layers what we observe.
  • Noise - an ever-present foe.
  • Mammogram - for image transfer to breast cancer specialists.
  • Radar view of Earth - as seen from the ERS synthetic aperture radar satellites.
  • Architectural backbone - the edges of an image.
  • Earth observation - urban and rural landscapes.
  • Jetstream - superimposed environmental fluctuations.
  • Predicting the S&P 500 index - financial engineering, using the elasticity of time.
  • Web middleware engineering - understanding telecommunications traffic.
  • Text and hypertext - information discovery and retrieval.
  • Cluster analysis - tackling the curse of dimensionality in multivariate data analysis.
  • Further astronomical examples - X-ray image filtering, deconvolution.
  • Geographic information systems - US agricultural and financial policy-making.
  • AFM images of semiconductor quantum dots - counting using mr_detect.
  • Integrated deconvolution and vision modeling
  • Microstructure of concrete - speckle removal from SEM images.

  • Notes on compression - again, what makes this approach both effective and efficient.
